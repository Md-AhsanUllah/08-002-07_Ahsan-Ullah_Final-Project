About Us

Barishal University Photographic Society started its journey in 2017 with some passionate photo enthusiasts. We are a community of passionate photographers. Our mission is to explore the art of photography and to help our members improve their skills through collaboration, events, and learning opportunities. Barishal University Photographic Society (BUPS) is an enthusiastic and dynamic community within the University of Barishal, Bangladesh, dedicated to nurturing and promoting the art of photography. Established by students and faculty members with a passion for capturing moments through the lens, BUPS provides a platform for budding photographers to express their creativity, hone their skills, and explore the diverse world of visual storytelling.

Objectives

The primary goal of the Barishal University Photographic Society is to cultivate a love for photography among its members and the broader university community. It aims to:

  • Develop skills:Organizing workshops, training sessions, and photography classes to help students develop both technical and artistic skills.
  • Foster collaboration:Encourage collaboration among students, faculty, and external photographers through various events and projects.
  • Showcase talent:Provide members with opportunities to showcase their work through exhibitions, contests, and university publications.
  • Create awareness:Use photography as a tool for social awareness, capturing images that reflect cultural, social, and environmental issues.

Activities

BUPS conducts a wide range of activities throughout the academic year to engage its members and the university at large:

  • Workshops and Training Sessions:From beginner-level camera handling to advanced techniques like portrait and landscape photography, BUPS offers hands-on training from experienced photographers.
  • Photo Walks and Field Trips:Regularly organized trips to scenic or culturally significant locations, these walks encourage members to practice photography in real-world settings.
  • Photography Exhibitions:BUPS hosts exhibitions on-campus to display the best work from its members, allowing them to gain exposure and feedback from peers and professionals.
  • Competitions and Contests: Inter-university and intra-university photography contests are held to spark creativity and healthy competition among students.
  • Collaborative Projects:BUPS members often collaborate on projects like photo documentaries, digital storytelling, and themed photo series that highlight significant topics like heritage, environment, and student life.
